PERMIT AQCUISITION

We help you obtain all the necessary construction permits and approvals required to start building in Kenya. This includes building permits, change-of-use approvals, subdivision permits, and occupation certificates from county governments such as Nairobi and Kiambu. Our team manages the application, submission, and follow-up—ensuring your project is legally approved and ready to proceed without unnecessary delays.

REGULATORY COMPLIANCE

Our experts ensure that your project complies with all Kenyan construction regulations, including zoning laws, environmental compliance (NEMA), public health approvals, and county building codes. We proactively identify compliance risks and resolve them before they become costly delays. Whether you’re a developer, contractor, or landowner, we keep your project aligned with legal requirements at every stage.

CONSULTATION SERVICES

We offer strategic construction compliance consultations to guide developers, investors, architects, and engineers through the legal aspects of the building process. Our team provides tailored advice on permit requirements, project feasibility, document preparation, and county regulations—helping you avoid pitfalls and plan with clarity.

End-to-End Support for Hassle-Free Construction Compliance

Title Deed Transfers

We manage the legal and administrative processes required to transfer land ownership from one party to another. Our team ensures that all documentation is verified, submitted, and followed up with the relevant land registry offices to prevent delays and disputes.

Contact Us

Change of User Applications

We facilitate Change of User approvals, allowing property owners to legally alter the designated land use (e.g., from residential to commercial). We handle the full application process from the preparation of justification reports to county-level approvals.

Contact Us

Architectural Plans

We work with licensed architects to prepare detailed architectural plans required for development approval. These plans comply with local building codes and regulations and are tailored to your project scope.

Contact Us

Architectural County Approvals

We submit your architectural plans to the relevant county planning office for review and approval. Our team ensures that all documentation is complete and adheres to county development control guidelines.

Contact Us

NEMA Licenses

We oversee the acquisition of Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA) licenses from the National Environment Management Authority (NEMA), ensuring that your project complies with environmental regulations.

Contact Us

NCA Licenses

We facilitate project registration and contractor accreditation with the National Construction Authority (NCA). This is required for all legal construction projects in Kenya.

Contact Us

Structural Designs

We coordinate the development of structural engineering drawings that meet both safety standards and regulatory requirements. These designs ensure the integrity of your building during construction and occupancy.

Contact Us

Structural County Approvals

We handle the submission and approval of structural drawings to the county government’s engineering departments, ensuring the designs meet local structural safety requirements.

Contact Us

Land Survey Services

We engage licensed land surveyors to provide accurate measurements, boundary definitions, and topographical surveys essential for planning and compliance.
