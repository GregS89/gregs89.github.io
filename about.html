How we started.

Mara Trust is a philanthropic lead community-based organization (CBO) that was established in the year of 2020. Our governance structure is predicated upon the stewardship of the local community, and we heavily depend on the counsel and guidance of local advisors and colleagues. We hold the fundamental belief that every individual possesses an inherent entitlement to their human rights, gender equality, and collective leadership. We recognize the importance of indigenous methods of acquiring knowledge and learning, and we duly acknowledge their significant role in the well-being of the global community. It is our esteemed privilege to collaborate with committed leaders and organizations who devote their lives to the progression and rejuvenation of local culture, traditional and innovative indigenous educational systems, holistic methods of caring for and safeguarding forests, waterways, communities, territories, and indigenous responses to climate change. Ultimately, we value enduring relationships, innovative ideas, and the act of accompanying others in providing service and benevolence to Mother Earth.
